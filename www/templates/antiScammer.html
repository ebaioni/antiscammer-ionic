Some friendly markdown We require access to your address book in order to add a new contact called "Scam Warning". The idea is to help you recognise known phone scammers so you won't share your details if you answer the call.

Every effort is made to keep this list up to date but there is no guarantee that scammers won't start using new phone numbers. If you have received a call from a scammer, you can submit it in an anonymous way and we'll add it to the list. Help us stop them!
